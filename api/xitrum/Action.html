Action

trait Action extends RequestEnv with SessionEnv with Logger with Net with Filter with BasicAuth with Redirect with UrlFor with Renderer with Responder with I18n

Action is designed to be separated from ActionActor. ActionActor extends Action. An ActionActor can pass its Action things outside without violating the principle of Actor: Do not leak Actor internals to outside.
